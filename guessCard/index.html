How to Play

Game Overview

Guess the daily Hearthstone card! You have unlimited attempts to guess the correct card. After each guess, you'll see how close your guess was to the target card across different attributes. The card set of the card to guess is already provided below the search bar!

Card Attributes

📛 Name
The card's full name
💎 Mana Cost
The card's mana cost (0-10+)
🏹 Classes
Class the card belongs to (or Neutral)
📊 Card Type
Minion, Spell, Weapon, Hero, Location, etc.
✨ Rarity
Common, Rare, Epic, or Legendary
📦 Card Set
The expansion set the card is from

Features

  • ⏰ New card available daily at midnight
  • 🔥 Track your daily streak of correct guesses
  • 💾 Your progress is automatically saved

Color Guide

🟢 Exact Match
Your guess matches the target card exactly
🟡 Partial Match
Your guess is partially correct or close to the target
🔴 No Match
Your guess doesn't match the target card
